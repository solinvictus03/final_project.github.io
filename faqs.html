FAQs

How will I know you received my order?

You'll receive an email confirming that your order has been received. If you do not receive an email confirmation, please check to see if the email address on your order was entered correctly.

How do your customers get superpowers?

Superpowers are obtained with compound V.Compound V is an alchemical super serum that was created by Frederick Vought.The serum was formulated to enhance the human body and mind.In modern times, the serum continues to be manufactured by Vought International geneticists to transform ordinary humans into extraordinary "heroes".

Are these drugs harmful to the human body?

Our products are developed by the world's smartest scientists and tested on a variety of experimental animals. We carefully check each drug in order to avoid consequences.

Can I return or exchange my purchase if I don't like it?

If the buyer changes his mind about using the purchased drug, the buyer has the right to contact technical support to return the goods.

If I got useful super powers, can I join the seven?

To join the ranks of the seven, you need to apply on our website. After consideration of the application, you will be invited to the competition, where you can show your abilities. After the judges at the competition will judge you and decide whether you are worthy of joining the ranks of the seven. But do not be discouraged if you are not accepted, you can safely cooperate with us. We will help you find a job for a superhero.
